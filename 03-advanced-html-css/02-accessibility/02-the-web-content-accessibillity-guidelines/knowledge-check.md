**Question: What is the purpose of the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines?**

**Answer:** The purpose of WCAG is to provide a single, shared international standard for web accessibility.

**Why:** They act as a detailed tool with specific rules (success criteria) to help developers build websites that are usable by people with disabilities.

**Question: What are the 4 principles of the WCAG?**

**Answer:** The four principles are **P**erceivable, **O**perable, **U**nderstandable, and **R**obust.

**Why:** These principles (known as POUR) are the foundation of accessibility. They mean content must be in a form users can perceive (see/hear), interact with, comprehend, and be reliably interpreted by assistive technologies.

**Remember:** Just **POUR** the accessibility on your website.
